Phone 999 for an ambulance

Your answers mean you don't need an emergency ambulance straightaway.

The 999 operator will ask:

  • where you are - the area or what’s nearby
  • what phone number they can call you back on if you get cut off
  • what’s happened
  • more about your symptoms - If you don't need an ambulance you'll get advice on what to do instead

Deaf people should use 18000 to contact 999 using text relay.
