Call 111 and speak to a nurse

The phone service will be very busy but you must wait to speak to someone. Tell 111 that you are calling about coronavirus symptoms.

Your symptoms suggest that you might have a coronavirus infection.

The nurse will:

  • ask you more about your symptoms
  • tell you what to do next

You need to stay away from other people until you have spoken to 111.

Read more self-isolation advice on NHS.UK

To protect others, do not go to a GP, pharmacy or hospital

Get an isolation note to give to your employer

You can send an isolation note to your employer as proof you need to stay off work because of coronavirus.

You do not need to get a note from a GP.
