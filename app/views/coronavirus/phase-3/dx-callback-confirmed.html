What if I don’t get a call back within XX hours?

You should get a call back within this time. If you don’t, you should call 111 and tell them you had booked a call with 111 online and have coronavirus symptoms.

What if miss the call?

A nurse will try to call up to 3 times. They will leave a message to say they have tried to call you, but you won’t be able to return their call. If you miss all of the calls, you need to start again using the online service or call 111 and say you have coronavirus symptoms.

What if I feel worse while I am waiting?

Call 111 and tell them you booked a call back online, but you feel worse while you are waiting. Tell them you have coronavirus symptoms.
