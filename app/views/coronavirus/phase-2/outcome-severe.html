Call NHS 111 to speak to a nurse

The phone service will be very busy, but you must wait until you can speak to someone.

Tell 111 that you are calling about coronavirus. You must tell them where you have travelled and about any contact you have had with someone with symptoms.

111 will arrange a test for you if you need one.

You should keep yourself separate from others in your household (self-isolate) until you have spoken to 111. Read more details about self-isolation.

To protect yourself and others, do not go to a GP, pharmacy or hospital
