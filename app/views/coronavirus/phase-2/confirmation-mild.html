Your details have been sent

You will be contacted within 24 hours to arrange a swab test.

Call 111 if you do not hear from the testing centre within 24 hours.

What happens during the test?

A sample will be taken from your throat or nose using a cotton bud. The sample is then sent to a lab for analysis.

Make sure you keep yourself separate from others in your household (self-isolate) until you have been contacted by the testing service. Read more details about self-isolation.

What to do if you start to feel worse

Call 111 and tell them you think you have coronavirus symptoms.

The phone service will be very busy, but you must wait until you get through.

To protect yourself and others, do not go to a GP, pharmacy or hospital.
