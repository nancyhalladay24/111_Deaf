You need to isolate yourself and stay indoors

If you’re on public transport, go home by the most direct route. Stay at least 2 metres away from people if you can.

If you’re away from home:

  • find a room where you can close the door
  • avoid touching people, surfaces and objects

To protect yourself and others, do not go to a GP, pharmacy or hospital

You should keep yourself separate from others in your household (self-isolate) until you have spoken to 111. Read more details about self-isolation.

Book a call with a 111 nurse

A nurse needs to speak to you to get you the right help.

They’ll talk to you about your symptoms and arrange a coronavirus test for you if you need one.

You should get a call within 24 hours.
