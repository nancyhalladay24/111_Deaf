COVID-19

Getting tested and managing symptoms at home

NHS.UK has information to help if you have symptoms. You can find out:

  • how long to isolate for if you or someone you live with has symptoms
  • how to manage your symptoms at home and what to do if you feel worse

To protect others, do not go to a GP, pharmacy or hospital

Ask for a test to check if you have coronavirus

The test will show if you have the virus now.

People in hospital, NHS staff and key workers are getting priority, but anyone with symptoms can apply.

If you can’t get a test straightaway you should try again later.

You can get the test by either:

  • going to a drive-through testing centre if you have a car (you can’t get a taxi)
  • getting a home test kit sent to you
Ask for a coronavirus test

Other things you can do on NHS.UK

You can also:

  • get an isolation note (like a sick note) to give to your employer if you need time off work
  • help the NHS plan its response to coronavirus by telling us about your current experience of the virus

Go to NHS.UK for more coronavirus information
