Get NHS text messages while you are isolating

The NHS would like to send you a text message each day while you are staying at home (self-isolating) with coronavirus symptoms.

The texts will ask you how you are feeling, and offer advice for looking after yourself.

Sharing your details also means we know where in the country people have symptoms.

You will be helping us to deal with the outbreak, and use NHS services, more effectively.

You can opt out at any time.
